# Audience Analysis – Productivity & AI Lifestyle Video

## Video Topic
"I Asked ChatGPT to Run My Day – Here’s What Happened"

## Target Audience
- Students
- Early professionals
- People struggling with time management and productivity

## Audience Pain Points
- Feeling busy but not productive
- Poor daily planning
- Constant distractions
- Difficulty prioritizing tasks
- Stress due to unfinished work

## Audience Goals
- Manage time better
- Stay focused throughout the day
- Complete important tasks on time
- Reduce stress and overthinking
- Use AI tools effectively in daily life

## Content Preferences
- Simple and practical advice
- Real-life experiments with AI
- Honest results (what worked and what didn’t)
- Clear step-by-step explanations
- Short, actionable takeaways

## Key Insight
This audience is motivated by content that shows how AI can be
used as a practical assistant to simplify daily routines rather
than replace human decision-making.
